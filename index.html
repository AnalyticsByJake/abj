Hi, I’m Jake — a detail-driven data and operations specialist who loves turning chaos into clean systems and raw data into real insight.

With a background in managing complex projects, streamlining processes, and editing everything from technical documentation to team workflows, I’ve built a career on clarity: clear communication, clear information, clear results. Whether I’m working in SQL, Excel, or Tableau, my goal is the same — to help teams work smarter and make decisions backed by well-organized, well-understood data.

I’ve created dashboards on housing trends, designed systems that keep people (and data) on track, and developed a serious appreciation for a well-structured spreadsheet. I enjoy simplifying complexity, finding the story in the numbers, and building tools that make work feel less like... well, work.

When I’m not refining a process or querying a database, I’m probably copious amounts of coffee or playing an intense game of "What's in your mouth?!" with my dog.

Let’s connect — especially if you're looking for someone who blends communication, coordination, and analytical thinking with a bit of humor and a lot of precision.

SQL Meets Suburbia

Ever wondered what insights lie behind Zillow’s mountain of real estate listings? This project tackles that question by transforming raw CSVs into a structured SQL database and then into interactive Tableau dashboards. From price trends to school proximity, I used MySQL and Tableau to explore the dynamics of the U.S. housing market — one query at a time.

Selling to America

Retail Insights with Excel's Full Power

Flip on the news and you’ll likely hear talk of China challenging America’s economic crown. Sometimes, less really is more — especially in Excel. In this project, I dive into a lean yet telling dataset simulating Walmart customer behavior. With limited fields and a dash of creativity, I assembled a full suite of analysis, KPI summaries, and dashboards to extract insight from the everyday. The goal? Show how even smaller datasets can pack a big punch when paired with the right structure and logic.
